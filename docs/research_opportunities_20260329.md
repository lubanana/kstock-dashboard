## ₿ 암호화폐 기회

### 1. Funding Rate Arbitrage
- **전략:** 선물/현물 펀딩비 차이를 이용한 무위험 수익
- **위험도:** Low
- **기대 수익:** 5-15% annual
- **구현 방법:** Monitor Binance, Bybit funding rates every 8 hours

### 2. Volume Profile Break
- **전략:** 고거래량 POC(Point of Control) 돌파 전략
- **위험도:** Medium
- **기대 수익:** 20-40% per trade
- **구현 방법:** Identify high volume nodes, wait for breakout with volume

### 3. Liquidation Cascade
- **전략:** 대규모 청산 시 반등 매매
- **위험도:** High
- **기대 수익:** 10-30% per trade
- **구현 방법:** Monitor liquidation heatmap, counter-trade extreme levels

### 4. On-Chain Analysis
- **전략:** 체인상 대형 지갑(고래) 동향 추적
- **위험도:** Medium
- **기대 수익:** 15-50% per cycle
- **구현 방법:** Track exchange inflows/outflows, whale wallet movements

### 5. Volatility Expansion
- **전략:** 변동성 수축 후 확장 구간 진입
- **위험도:** Medium
- **기대 수익:** 20-60% per trade
- **구현 방법:** Bollinger Bands squeeze + volume spike confirmation

## 🔬 연구 방법론

### Technical Analysis

- Volume Spike Detection: 2.5x average volume
- Breakout Patterns: 5-day high breakout
- Moving Average Cross: Golden/Death cross
- RSI Divergence: Price/RSI divergence
- Bollinger Bands: Squeeze and expansion

### Quantitative Screening

- Value Score: PER, PBR, ROE based scoring
- Momentum Score: Price change over 20/60 days
- Quality Score: Debt ratio, FCF stability
- Risk Score: Volatility, drawdown analysis

### Market Microstructure

- Order Flow: Bid/ask imbalance
- Tick Analysis: Up/down volume ratio
- VWAP Deviation: Mean reversion signals
- Time of Day: Opening/closing patterns

### Alternative Data

- News Sentiment: NLP-based sentiment analysis
- Social Media: Twitter/Reddit mention volume
- Insider Trading: Executive buying/selling
- Sector Rotation: Industry momentum shifts
